My Interests and Disinterests

Below is a list of things that I like and dislike. I figured that if I had to write about things that I like, I might as well make a list of things that I dislike as well. It's easier to write about your hatred with a passion, rather than to write about things that you like. But nevertheless, this list was an enjoyable one to make :)

What I Like

  • Simple Yet Special Things

    I like things that are simple, yet special. I believe that everyone is unique in their own way, so there isn't a need for you to copy anyone's style. It's the same for designs and ideas too. Each of us thinks in a different and special way, so we will formulate different ideas based on what we know. So, why copy when you can be special and unique?

  • Stories

    Personally, I've written many stories, but most of them were inspired by the stories that I hear everyday. Throughout the years, I've realised that I can't stand games that don't have a clear plot or rich plot. They seem to be the only things that keep the games going (or, at least, keeps me playing the game). So, unless the game has a wonderful plot, don't bother getting me to try it :P

  • Amazing/Inspiring People

    I've always admired people who dare to chase their dreams and go against conventional thinking. They are really courageous to take on challenges that most would avoid and it is that bravery that inspires me to work towards my goals no matter how ridiculous they may be.

  • My School

    If I am given the chance to go back into the past and make a decision again, I would still apply to my current school, Dunman High. I like the school culture and its focus on the character development of the students. To me, a person's character is of utmost importance while the rest is secondary. And the fact that Dunmanians actually live up to their name makes me feel really proud to be one. Most of the people here are awesome too, if you get to know them :) And I'm glad that I managed to form many friendships here in Dunman High :D

What I Hate

  • I can't stand being alone

    I guess you can call it a fear or something, but if I am ever alone, I get bouts of depression and I will start to feel fear settling in. Even when I'm in a crowd, I get lonely easily if I don't associate myself with anyone there. I'm sure that everyone's afraid of being lonely, but as for me, loneliness is probably a fear that I can never overcome.

  • I hate hypocrites

    Just because you want to look good in front of other people doesn't mean you have to pretend to be nice. I hate it when people say something in front of one person and say another in front of the next. It's called double-crossing and it reflects the lack of integrity and sincerity in you. And that's what irks me... =.=

  • I hate people who look down upon others

    You may be better than average, but that doesn't give you the right to look down on other people and deprive them of the opportunity to achieve excellence. It just reflects how much you want to stay at the top of your game while you prevent others from surpassing you. The worst thing is when you mock others for their lack of ability while you lack the most basic form of respect. Thus, you don't deserve any respect at all.

  • I hate it when girls allow themselves to be stereotyped

    Some girls deliberately show themselves as weak individuals who always need help in the most trivial of situations. The worst thing is when they do so just to attract attention to themselves. But they are actually conforming themselves to societal expectations of what being a female is about. Perhaps my hatred for such people is also due to the fact that they are not being original at all. Hmm...
